Vedafit Account Deletion

If you want to delete your Vedafit account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: vedafitofficial@gmail.com
  2. Use subject: Delete my Vedafit account
  3. Include your registered email address

Your account and all associated data will be deleted within 48 hours.

Data deleted:

  • Profile info
  • Email
  • Fitness logs
  • App usage data
